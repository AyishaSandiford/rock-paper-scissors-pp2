Welcome to Rock Paper Scissors

How to play

Rock Paper Scissors requires 2 players


Here you will play against the computer. This is a game of chance. After you make a selection the computer will make it's choice. Both selections will be compared and base on the rules a winner will be determined

Instructions

To get started, go to the home screen and click the Start Button.
Once on the game screen, you can start your play. Choose Rock, Paper or Scissors to make your selection. The computer will immediately choose its selection. It will be displayed who won that round.
Each winner of a round is awarded 1 point. If the round ties, no points are awarded to either player

The first player to 5 points wins the game.


Rules

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock
