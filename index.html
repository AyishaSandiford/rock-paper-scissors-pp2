Welcome to Rock Paper Scissors

How to play

Rock Paper Scissors requires 2 players.


Here you will play against the computer. This is a game of chance. After you make a selection the computer will make a random choice. Both selection will be compared and a winner will be determined


Instructions

To get started, go to the home screen and click the Start Button.
Once on the game screen, you can play. Choose Rock, Paper or Scissors to make your selection. The computer will immediately choose their selection and then you will see who won that round.
Each round winner is awarded 1 point. If the round ties, no points are awarded


The first to 5 points wins the game.

Rules

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock
